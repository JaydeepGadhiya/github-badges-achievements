# ⚡ Get Your FIRST Achievement — right here, in 10 seconds!

> New to achievements? Earn **Quickdraw ⚡** using *this very repo*. All you do is
> **open an issue and close it within 5 minutes** — we'll do it in ~10 seconds. 🚀

| Difficulty | Time | Badge you'll earn |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 🟢 Super Easy | ~10 sec | ⚡ Quickdraw |

---

## 🪜 Step-by-step

1. ⭐ **Star this repo** (optional, but it helps others find it!).

2. 🐞 Go to the **[Issues tab](../../issues)** of this repo → click **New issue**.
   - If you see a template called **"⚡ My First Achievement"**, pick it. Otherwise choose *"Open a blank issue."*

3. 📝 Give it a title like:
   ```
   My first achievement 🎉
   ```
   Then click **Submit new issue**.

4. ⏱️ **Immediately** click the **Close issue** button (near the comment box).
   - Do it within a few seconds — you have up to **5 minutes**, but 10 seconds looks cooler. 😎

5. 🎉 **That's it!** Head to your profile — the **Quickdraw ⚡** badge appears in your
   **Achievements** panel (usually within minutes, sometimes up to an hour).

---

## 🖼️ What it looks like

```
  New issue  ──▶  Submit  ──▶  Close issue   (⏱️ under 5:00)
     🐞            📝            ✅  =  ⚡ Quickdraw!
```

## ❓ Not showing up?

- Give it a few minutes and **refresh your profile**. ⏳
- Make sure the **Achievements panel is on**:
  **Settings → Public profile → ✅ Show Achievements on my profile.**
- You must be **logged in as yourself** when you open + close the issue. 🔑

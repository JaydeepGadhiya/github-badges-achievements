# 🧠 Galaxy Brain

> **Goal:** Get your **answers accepted** in GitHub Discussions.

| Difficulty | Time | Tiers |
|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 🟡 Medium | ~15 min | 2 → 🥉 8 → 🥈 16 → 🥇 32 accepted answers |

---

## ✅ What counts
- You answer a question in a repo's **Discussions**, and the asker marks your reply as the **accepted answer**.
- The discussion must be in the **"Q&A"** category (that's the one with accepted answers).

## 🪜 Step-by-step

1. **Enable Discussions** on a repo you own:
   **Settings → General → Features → ✅ Discussions**. 💬
2. Create a discussion in the **Q&A** category (e.g. *"How do I …?"*).
3. From **another account** (a friend's, or a second account you own), post an **answer** to that question.
4. As the question owner, click **Mark as answer** ✔️ on that reply.
5. The account whose answer was accepted earns progress toward the badge. 🧠

## 💡 Tips
- The most rewarding route: genuinely answer questions in active open-source projects that use Q&A Discussions.
- You need **2 accepted answers** to unlock; 8 / 16 / 32 for the tiers.

## ⏳ When it appears
Minutes to a few hours after your 2nd accepted answer.

# ⚡ Quickdraw

> **Goal:** Close an **issue or pull request within 5 minutes** of opening it.

| Difficulty | Time | Tiers |
|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 🟢 Easy | ~2 min | Single tier (no levels) |

---

## ✅ What counts
- Opening an **issue** and closing it within 5 minutes, **or**
- Opening a **PR** and closing/merging it within 5 minutes.
- Works on your **own** repo — no one else needed.

## 🪜 Step-by-step (fastest method)

1. Go to any repo you own → **Issues** tab. 🐞
2. Click **New issue** → add a title like `test issue` → **Submit new issue**.
3. Immediately click **Close issue**. ⏱️
4. Done — you did it in well under 5 minutes! ✅

> 🧠 The timer starts when the issue/PR is **created** and must be closed before 5:00 elapses.

## 💡 Tips
- If Issues are disabled on the repo, enable them: **Settings → General → Features → Issues**.
- A merged fast PR also counts, so you may earn this while going for 🦈 Pull Shark.

## ⏳ When it appears
Usually within minutes.

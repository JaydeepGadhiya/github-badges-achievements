# 💖 Public Sponsor

> **Goal:** Sponsor a developer or organization through **GitHub Sponsors** — publicly.

| Difficulty | Time | Tiers |
|:---:|:---:|:---|
| 🟡 Medium ($) | ~5 min | Single tier (no levels) |

---

## ✅ What counts
- Sponsoring **any** account that has GitHub Sponsors enabled.
- Your sponsorship must be **public** (not private) for the badge.
- Even the **smallest tier** (often ~$1/month) works. 💵

## 🪜 Step-by-step

1. Add a payment method: **Settings → Billing and plans**. 💳
2. Find a profile with a **Sponsor** button (many maintainers have one).
3. Click **Sponsor** → choose a tier (the lowest is fine). 🎁
4. **Important:** keep the visibility set to **Public**, not private. 👀
5. Confirm the sponsorship — badge unlocked! 💖

## 💡 Tips
- Sponsor a project you actually use — it directly supports open source. 🌍
- You can cancel later; the badge is tied to having sponsored publicly.

## ⏳ When it appears
Usually within minutes of the first public sponsorship.
